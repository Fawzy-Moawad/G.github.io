Women’s Club Goal

We are a dedicated team of five passionate ladies, committed to enriching the lives of Egyptian families in our community. Our mission is to organize events that are not only beneficial but also entertaining for everyone.
We plan a diverse range of activities, from exclusive ladies-only gatherings to fun-filled events for kids, and most importantly, inclusive events for the entire family. Our creative minds are brimming with ideas that we are eager to bring to life. We believe in the power of community and are constantly striving to foster a sense of togetherness through our initiatives.
Your feedback and suggestions are invaluable to us, as they help us tailor our events to better serve our community's needs.
Whether it’s a cultural celebration, a family picnic, or a community workshop, we aim to create memorable experiences that bring joy and unity to our members.
Join us in building a supportive, vibrant, and connected community. Together, we can make a difference and create lasting bonds.
We welcome any helpful comments and suggestions that can help us achieve our goal of bringing our community closer together.
Let's work hand-in-hand to make our events a success and our community stronger.
For any enquiries or suggestions, please contact us at women@eccsbc.com.

ECCS BC

The Egyptian Canadian Cultural Society of BC, ECCS BC, is a dynamic non-profit organization gathering and serving all BC resident Egyptians who have genuine interest in the Egyptian Mosaic Culture, Traditions, History and Future.
