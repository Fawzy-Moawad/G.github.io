Youth Club

Our Youth Club is driven by young members who bring fresh ideas and boundless energy to the table. As part of this vibrant group, you'll find that the possibilities for activities are limitless. We're thinking beyond traditional gatherings like picnics; envision exciting adventures such as snowboarding, parachuting, and other thrilling experiences. While we also enjoy more common activities like bowling, our aim is to push the boundaries and think big.
We welcome all innovative ideas and are eager to collaborate with you to turn your concepts into real events. Whether it's a daring outdoor adventure or a creative workshop, you'll find many enthusiastic members ready to dive into new challenges alongside you. If you have a great idea, work with us â€“ who knows, we might gather a group of 30 participants instead of just 5.
Please note, due to safety regulations, our activities are not suitable for children. We love kids, but our events in this category are designed for youth and young adults.
Keep an eye on ECCS announcements for family-friendly events specifically tailored for children. The Youth Club is more than just a place for activities; it's a community where you can meet new people and form lasting connections. Being far from home in Canada, we strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment that feels like family.
Whether you're a newcomer seeking support or someone who's just discovering ECCS, know that there's a special place for you in our circle. Join us in making the Youth Club a dynamic and inclusive space where everyone can thrive and feel at home.
For any enquiries or to share your ideas, please contact us at youth@eccsbc.com.
