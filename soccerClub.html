Soccer Club

The Egyptian Canadian Cultural Society of BC ECCSBC is proud to present the Egyptian Soccer Club ESC as part of our vibrant community activities. The ESC is entirely run by dedicated volunteers and serves as a welcoming space for all Egyptians and Arab residents of BC who share a passion for soccer.
Our club is open to all soccer enthusiasts aged 14 and up.
We offer opportunities for everyone to join in, whether you're looking to enjoy casual games, participate in practice sessions, or engage in friendly matches. We utilize both turf and grass fields where available, and during the fall and winter seasons, we play indoors in gym facilities. We invite you to come and register with us, and become part of a fun and dynamic soccer community.
For enquiries and registration, please contact us at soccer@eccsbc.com. Join us and experience the joy of soccer while building friendships and staying active.
